The Sauce Care Package

Main Page

I recently was gifted this box of hot sauces from the show "HotOnes" on YouTube season 9. The sauces are packaged by a company that groups the hot sauces together and delivers them to customers. The company is called Heatonist, and they specialize in promoting hot sauces.

Sauce Bae | Skinny Habanero Hot Sauce

Ingredients: Pineapple, water, white vinegar, habaneros, onion, garlic, jalapenos, turmeric, salt, cumin, black pepper, ginger

The first sauce in the picture pairs well with avocado toast and chicken wraps.

Shaquanda's | Hot Pepper Sauce

Ingredients: Onions, white vinegar, apple cider vinegar, chili peppers, tomato paste (tomato, citric acid) demerara sugar, lemon juice, sea salt, ginger juice, horseradish, mustard powder, turmeric powder

The second sauce in the picture pairs well with chicken and fish.

Lucky Dog | Year of the Dog

Ingredients: Pineapple, rice vinegar, onion, roasted garlic, honey, dried red chilies, toasted onion, sea salt, agave, lime juice, ginger, dried garlic, mustard flour, toasted sesame seeds

The third sauce in the picture pairs well with rice dishes, stir fry, and grilled chicken.
